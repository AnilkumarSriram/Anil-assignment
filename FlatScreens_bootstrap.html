SAMSUNG LED TV

Review By: Anil Sriram
Buy this product

Review Page

Cost:600

Specification

TV Size: 46inch

Resolution: 4k

Refresh Rate:120HZ

LEDVA

3D

Description


The Samsung JS9000 isn’t just the best Samsung LED TV we’ve seen this year, it’s the best TV we've seen, period. It has pretty deep, nicely uniform blacks, so it’s a good option for movie lovers. It has very little blur and not much of the unpleasant fogginess that you get with some LED TVs, so sports will really shine. It’s also good and responsive, so gamers won’t need to worry about delay to the actions they’re trying to perform. You also get some of the new extras that the major brands are pushing, like the ability to display a wider range of colors, and HDR, which lets bright elements in a picture get extra bright. The main downside to this TV is that its colors begin to fade at wide angles. This is a common issue for LED TVs, but still an annoyance for some people. You’ll also need to find room for the included One Connect box, as the TV doesn’t work without that attached. SUHD TVs are pricey, and the curved screen adds even more to the already substantial price tag. If you’re looking for the absolute best TV money can buy, though, we haven’t found anything that tops this set in 2015
